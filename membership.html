FAQ

Common questions

Is there a membership fee?

Yes, in two parts. One-time joining fee — pick either ₹2400 lifetime or ₹300 per year. Plus, monthly ₹50 — mandatory for every active member, regardless of joining plan. So most members pay either ₹2400 + ₹50/month, or ₹300/year + ₹50/month.

I’m a lifetime member — do I still pay monthly?

Yes. Lifetime ₹2400 covers your joining fee for good — no renewals on that. But the ₹50/month is a separate, ongoing contribution that every active member pays. It funds the day-to-day: ground prep, water, equipment, match-day costs.

Do I need experience?

Not at all. We care about commitment more than skill. Both will be tested.

What documents do I need?

A clear photo, and ID proof (Aadhar / PAN). IDs are stored privately and used only to verify identity.

How long does approval take?

Typically 2–5 days. You will get an email either way.

Can I still attend matches if not selected?

Yes. The stands are always open. Bring the village.

Can I switch plans later?

Yes. Email us at harichandanpurcricketclub@gmail.com and we’ll update your record.
