About the club

Where it began
and where we are headed.

Harichandanpur Club started as a few friends, a tape ball and one shared obsession with playing. Today it is the home turf for everyone in town who lives for the game.

Our story

Built on the streets. Forged in the field.

In 2024, a handful of sport-mad kids from Harichandanpur decided the village deserved its own team. No infrastructure, no funding, no fancy kits — just a will to play and a refusal to wait for permission.

What started with one bat and an old tennis ball is now a roster of dedicated players, a calendar full of fixtures and a community that turns up for every match. We are not the biggest club. We are the loudest.

This website is for our players, our supporters and anyone who wants to be a part of what we are building.

What we stand for

Three things, non-negotiable.

Discipline

Show up on time. Train when no one is watching. Respect the game, the opposition and your teammates.

Passion

Play every ball like the match depends on it. Celebrate every wicket. Get hurt, get up, get back out there.

Community

We win as one, lose as one. Harichandanpur is not a postal code — it is the crowd in our corner.
